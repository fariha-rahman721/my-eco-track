### Features of Eco-Track:
##### Real-time Environmental Monitoring:
Continuously tracks air quality, water quality, soil parameters, and temperature.
Displays live data using interactive charts and graphs for easy interpretation.

##### Advanced Data Analytics & Visualization:
Historical trends and predictive analytics help identify patterns and risks.
Customizable dashboards allow users to focus on metrics that matter most.

##### Sustainability Reporting:
Automatically generates detailed reports on environmental metrics.
Helps organizations comply with environmental regulations and sustainability goals.

##### Community Engagement & Collaboration:
Users can join eco-projects, participate in campaigns, and share insights.
Features forums and discussion boards for eco-conscious communities.

##### Mobile & Cross-Device Compatibility:
Fully responsive, works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
Access live environmental data and alerts anytime, anywhere.

##### Notifications & Alerts System:
Instant notifications for environmental thresholds or critical events.
Alerts can be customized by type, location, or severity.

##### Integration with IoT Devices & Sensors:
Connects with various IoT sensors to capture real-time environmental data.
Supports API integration for third-party tools and analytics platforms.

##### User Accounts & Personalized Profiles:
Users can create profiles to save preferences, track activities, and receive personalized insights.
Role-based access allows different levels of control for organizations and communities.
